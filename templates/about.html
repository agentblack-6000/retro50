The "Retrospective" Revision

What is a Retrospective Revision Timetable?

A retrospective revision timetable, in constrast to a regular revision timetable, "plans" to revise weaknesses, rather than to revise the next topic again and again. The process is as follows-

  1. Study session
  2. Log revision and rate proficiency
  3. Break
  4. During the next study session, look at the table, and decide what you want to revise
  5. Study session
  6. Log revision and rate proficiency
  7. Break
  8. Repeat!
