What counts as a revision-

You must apply your knownledge in some way, not just read notes, to log a revision in the retrospective revision timetable. A few examples of what counts as a revision are-

  1. Using the Feynman technique.
  2. Using the Blurting method.
  3. Solving question papers/problem sets/worksheets on the topic.
  4. Mind-mapping the concept
  5. Using active-recall, i.e asking yourself questions to recall concepts
